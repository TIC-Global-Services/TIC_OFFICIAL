Background

Our team of creative experts specializes in delivering comprehensive branding solutions, from eye-catching logos and cohesive visual identities to cutting-edge websites, mobile apps, and engaging multimedia content.

What we do

We are a concept driven brand design agency that helps companies build, communicate and strengthen their brand identities and ideas.

Philosophy

We always work in regards to the final output, be it an application, website, product etc. Since final output is where the identity should thrive and not in an impressive design presentation or PDF.

Clientele

We're proud to collaborate with a diverse range of clients from bold startups to established brands each bringing unique ideas to the table. Their trust fuels our creativity, and together, we turn visions into results.

Founder

Meet Pragya Muthuraman — the creative force and strategic mind powering TIC Global Services. From crafting stunning digital experiences to redefining what it means to build a brand online; and is all about turning big ideas into bigger realities.

With over 50 successful projects under our belt this year, she doesn't just lead the company—she set the pace for innovation in 3D websites breaking the barriers of traditional web development, ensuring every project is as bold and original as the clients we serve. Whether it's web branding, app design, or exploring new frontiers Pragya is always looking for the next big challenge.

Collaboration is key

TIC was built on the belief that successful branding comes from a collaborative process. We work closely with our clients, ensuring they feel confident and supported at every stage of building their brand.

Not just a logo

A logotype is undeniably important and plays a key role in our work, but many companies mistakenly view their logo, colors, and fonts as the entirety of their brand. While this can be true in some instances, it's often just a small part of what the audience experiences. In fact, a well-designed landing page can communicate much more than a logo alone.

Building strong brands with final output in focus

If a website falls short, no matter how impressive the guidelines or design presentations are, the consumer won't engage with them. While we use design presentations to help convey the concept and the broader vision, our priority is always on what the end user will experience. Instead of following the traditional approach.

we reverse the process by starting with the final product and concluding with the guidelines.

This is our
process

Our philosophy is that a brand comes to life through its output. To create the right concept and feel, we believe in starting from the right point, establishing it with the customer, and, most importantly, ensuring seamless communication with the client to avoid any gaps.

01. Startup

We guide you through the upcoming process, taking the time to get to know you and your brand better, so we can set clear goals for the final outcome. It's important for us to understand your current position, where you want to go, and if you have any references or ideas to share.

02. Moodboards

At this stage, we typically have a clear direction in mind. We align it with you, listening carefully to your input. By this stage, we've usually started outlining the final deliverable and are testing it internally to ensure it aligns with expectations.

03. Brand identity

We begin by researching the market, audience, and company values to understand what sets your brand apart. Then, we define your brand's positioning, personality, and key messages. Finally, we create a visual identity, including logo, colors, and typography, that reflects your brand's essence.

04. Wireframes

At this stage, we finalize the wireframes by setting the text and interactions. We work in shared documents to ensure full transparency throughout the process. If the customer requires external copywriting assistance, we have in house seo optimisation/ content writers 24/7.

05. Web Design

The face of your Brand would be your website, and we are happy to be a part of it.
